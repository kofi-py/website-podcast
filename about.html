About Us

Hi! Welcome to Bible Essentials with Kofi Adu-Boateng! This podcast is meant to help people understand the Bible in a much simpler way.

The Bible is the center of Christianity. It's God's words to us. However, many people find the Bible confusing and difficult to understand. This podcast aims to make the Bible simple by breaking down key concepts, stories and teachings in a way that's easy to grasp.

Here, you'll find all the episodes for free. Each episode covers a different topic, from the basics of who Jesus is to the meaning behind key Bible stories.
